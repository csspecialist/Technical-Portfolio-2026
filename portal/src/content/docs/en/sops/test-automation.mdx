## Overview

The company VPN uses **Cisco AnyConnect** as the client and **Duo Security** for multi-factor authentication (MFA). You need both before you can access internal resources remotely.

## Prerequisites

- A company-issued account with Active Directory (AD) credentials
- A smartphone with the **Duo Mobile** app installed and enrolled
- Internet access

:::note
If you have not yet enrolled your device in Duo, contact the IT Help Desk before proceeding.
:::

---

## Steps

### 1. Install Cisco AnyConnect

1. Go to the internal software portal or contact IT to obtain the **Cisco AnyConnect** installer for your operating system.
2. Run the installer and follow the on-screen prompts.
3. Restart your machine if prompted.

:::tip
Accept the default installation path. Custom paths may cause issues with future updates.
:::

---

### 2. Connect to the VPN Gateway

1. Open **Cisco AnyConnect Secure Mobility Client**.
2. In the connection field, enter `vpn.company.com`.
3. Click **Connect**.

---

### 3. Authenticate with AD Credentials

1. When prompted, enter your **Active Directory username** (typically in `firstname.lastname` or `DOMAIN\username` format).
2. Enter your **AD password**.
3. Click **OK**.

:::caution
Do not use your personal email address or a local machine password. Use the same credentials you use to log in to your work computer or Microsoft 365.
:::

---

### 4. Approve the Duo MFA Prompt

1. Once your AD credentials are accepted, a push notification is sent to your enrolled device via **Duo Mobile**.
2. Open the **Duo Mobile** app on your phone.
3. Tap **Approve**.

:::caution
You have **30 seconds** to approve the Duo push notification. If the request times out, the VPN connection attempt will fail. Return to Cisco AnyConnect and click **Connect** again to start a new session.
:::

---

## Result

After approval, Cisco AnyConnect displays a **Connected** status. You now have secure access to internal company resources.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Symptom | Likely Cause | Action |
|---|---|---|
| "Authentication failed" | Incorrect AD credentials | Verify your username and password |
| Duo push never arrives | Device not enrolled or no data connection | Check Duo enrollment; use a Wi-Fi or cellular connection |
| Connection times out immediately | Wrong VPN gateway address | Confirm the address is `vpn.company.com` |
| Push expires before you approve | Response took longer than 30 seconds | Reconnect and approve promptly |
